Quick answer Can you get legal aid?

Not what you're looking for?

Use this tool to check if you can get legal aid, which could help pay for legal advice, mediation or representation in court.

You'll be asked general questions about your legal problem and your financial situation.

What you need to know

Start now

  • Before you start

Using tool does not guarantee you'll get legal aid - it's just a guide.

It only deals with non-criminal ('civil') cases. If you've been accused of a crime, ask your solicitor or barrister if you can get legal aid.

If you're under 18, your eligibility for legal aid might depend on your parents' or guardians' income, depending on your problem.
